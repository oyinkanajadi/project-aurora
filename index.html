Project Aurora

Transforming the Patient Appointment Booking Journey at Aurora Health Group

Overview

Client: Aurora Health Group (Fictional)
Industry: Private Healthcare
Focus: Patient Access & Booking Optimisation

The Problem

  • Long wait times to secure appointments
  • Missed patient demand due to unanswered calls
  • Manual scheduling processes
  • High DNA (Did Not Attend) rates
  • Fragmented systems and duplicated data entry

Root Cause Insight

Analysis revealed that the primary issue was not demand, but the absence of a unified scheduling system. Manual triage across multiple channels created delays, increased administrative burden, and prevented real-time visibility of clinician capacity.

AS-IS Process

Appointment requests are received through phone, email, and web channels. Operations teams manually check clinician availability and coordinate bookings, leading to delays and inefficiencies.
